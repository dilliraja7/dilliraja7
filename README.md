> 
> **Tech Stack:** Python, MATLAB, CNN, LSTM

### 📄 TalentRAG-AI
> **An intelligent recruitment screening platform powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation.**
> 
> **Tech Stack:** Python, Streamlit, LangChain, FAISS, OpenAI  
> [![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20Demo-Vercel-blue?style=flat-square&logo=vercel)](https://ai-portfolio-teal-delta.vercel.app/)

* **Resume Screening:** Seamlessly automates CV filtering and matches profiles directly with target job roles.
* **Semantic Search:** Employs dense vector embeddings to search and retrieve matching resumes beyond simple keyword matches.
* **Skill Gap Analysis:** Automatically evaluates candidate profiles against job requirements to highlight crucial skill gaps.
* **Question Generation:** Dynamically generates targeted technical and soft-skill interview questions based on the candidate's resume context.
* **Resume-Job Matching:** Scores resume relevance relative to specific job descriptions with detailed analytics.
